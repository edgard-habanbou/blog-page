Football

Introduction

Football is a popular sport that brings people of all ages together. It's so powerful that it can even unite countries in events like the World Cup, where nations from around the world compete for the top prize.

History Of Football

Football in its current form arose in England in the middle of the 19th century. The first known ball game which also involved kicking took place In China in the 3rd and 2nd century BC under the name cuju. Cuju was played with a round ball (stitched leather with fur or feathers inside) on an area of a square.

World Cup

The FIFA World Cup is a tournament held every 4 years in a country picked by three phases:

  1. Bidding Phase
  2. Evaluation Process Phase
  3. Voting Process Phase
